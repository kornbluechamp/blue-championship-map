# Onsite Validation Checklist

## Confirm the official base map
- Ask whether the public 2026 course-map PDF is the current spectator map.
- Ask whether any facilities moved after the map was printed.
- Do not add volunteer-only evacuation details to the public version.

## Capture calibration anchors first
Save at least four widely separated **Calibration anchor** points:
1. Main admission gate or precise clubhouse entrance.
2. Holes 8/9/10 amenity junction.
3. Holes 12/13 amenity junction.
4. Hole 6 reference point.
5. An additional point near Holes 3/14 if convenient.

Stand still for 10-20 seconds. Prefer GPS accuracy under roughly 10-15 meters.

## Validate every public facility
For each location, record:
- Public-facing entrance, not the center of the tent/building.
- Nearest hole or landmark.
- Which side of the cart path.
- Public, hospitality-only, volunteer-only, or restricted.
- Accessible entrance or route, when applicable.
- Photo reference and direction the photo faces.

Categories to validate:
- Restrooms and accessible restrooms.
- First aid.
- Concessions.
- Water/refill stations.
- Main admission gate and exits.
- Expo Row entrances.
- Public viewing areas.
- Pro shop/merchandise.
- Hospitality entrances.
- Volunteer Headquarters.

## Record the route network
Record short segments between meaningful junctions rather than one giant route.
- Mark each route Allowed, Conditional, or Restricted.
- Note crossings that close while players hit.
- Note steep, difficult, or non-accessible segments.
- Record player-only, cart-only, hospitality-only, and dead-end paths as restrictions.

## High-priority routes
- Hole 10 to nearest restrooms/concessions.
- Hole 9 to nearest restrooms/concessions.
- Hole 6 to nearest restrooms/concessions.
- Hole 9/10 area toward clubhouse and first aid.
- Main gate to first aid.
- Main gate to Expo Row.
- Clubhouse/Fan Zone internal public walkway.

## End of visit
- Download `scouting-data.json`.
- Upload the JSON and photos back to ChatGPT.
- List any starter marker that is visibly wrong.
